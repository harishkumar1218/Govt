

### Question 1
What was the name of the Arab traveler who wrote extensively about Indian culture, art, and architecture in the 12th century?

**Options:**
A) Alberuni
B) Ibn Battuta
C) Marco Polo
D) Ibn Sina

**Answer:** A) Alberuni
**Explanation:** Alberuni was an Arab traveler and scholar who wrote about Indian culture, art, and architecture in his book "The Book of India". He visited India in the 12th century and documented various aspects of Indian society, including its art, architecture, and cultural practices.
